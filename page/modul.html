Simple Present Tense

tenses yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan kejadian yang terjadi secara teratur, berulang, atau rutinitas pada masa sekarang.

Formula:

(+) Positive
S + to be + Complement I am handsome. Noun / Kata Sifat S + V1 (+s/es) + Complement I live in Surabaya. Noun / Kata Sifat
(-) Negative
S + to be + not + Complement I am not handsome. Verb / Kata Kerja S + do/does + not + V1 + Complement I do not live in Surabaya. Verb / Kata Kerja
(?) Introgative
to be + S + Complement + ? Am I handsome? Noun / Kata Sifat Do/does + S + V1 + Complement + ? Do I live in Surabaya? Verb / Kata Kerja

Formula:

(+) Positive
S + to be (was/were) + Complement I was a driver back then. Noun / Kata Sifat S + V2 + Complement I slept at hotel last night. Verb / Kata Kerja
(-) Negative
S + to be (was/were) + not + Complement I was not a driver back then. Noun / Kata Sifat S + did + not + V1 + Complement I did not sleep at hotel last night. Verb / Kata Kerja
(?) Introgative
To be (was/were) + S + not + Complement + ? Was I a driver back then? Noun / Kata Sifat To be (was/were) + S + V1 + Complement + ? Was I sleep at hotel last night? Verb / Kata Kerja

Simple Future Tense

tenses yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan kejadian yang belum mulai dan baru terjadi di waktu yang akan datang atau masa depan.

Formula:

(+) Positive
You shall pick me up next night. I will come to your house tomorrow. S + (will/shall) + V1 + Complement We are going to meet at 08:00 PM this Sunday. She is going to go for a walk next morning. S + to be (is, am, are) + going to + V1
(-) Negative
You shall not pick me up next night. I will not come to your house tomorrow. S + (will/shall) + not + V1 + Complement We are not going to meet at 08:00 PM this Sunday. She is not going to go for a walk next morning. S + to be (is, am, are) + not + going to + V1
(?) Introgative
Shall you pick me up next night? Will you come to my house tomorrow? (Will/shall) ? S + V1 + Complement + ? Are We going to meet at 08:00 PM this Sunday? Is She going to go for a walk next morning? To be (is, am, are) + S + going to + V1 + ?
